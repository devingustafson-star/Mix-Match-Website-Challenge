The Gravity Thieves

On the moon of Xylos, the atmosphere is thin and the mountains are made of pure obsidian. Space Pirates have established hidden camps at the summits to hide from the Galactic Federation.

These pirates are experts at Mountain Climbing. They use jet-boosted boots to leap across massive canyons, searching for rare crystals hidden in the highest caves.
